# Kisaan Mitra 🌾

Improving Farmer's Quality of Life through sustainable, efficient, and affordable agricultural technology.

## 📖 Overview

Kisaan Mitra is a social-impact agritech initiative focused on the 89.4% of small farmers in India. Traditional farming equipment is often expensive to run, maintenance-heavy, and environmentally damaging. Our solution is a **Multi-functional EV Reaper** that cuts costs, enhances efficiency, and eliminates the carbon footprint of harvesting.

## The Problem

- **Extreme Conditions:** Laborious tasks in harsh weather affecting health.
- **Economic Burden:** High fuel and maintenance costs of IC engines.
- **Low Priority:** Small farmers are often ignored by industrial harvester services.
- **Environmental Impact:** Stubble burning and diesel emissions contributing to severe air pollution.

## 🚀 The Solution: Multi-functional EV Reaper

One piece of equipment, five essential farming operations. Our EV Reaper is designed for year-round utility, ensuring that a farmer's investment never sits idle.

- **Weeder:** Effortless weed removal without crop damage.
- **Tiller:** Efficient soil preparation for planting.
- **Sprayer:** Uniform application of fertilizers and pesticides.
- **Sowing Machine:** Precision seed planting.
- **Reaper:** High-speed harvesting with minimal yield loss.

## 🌍 Social & Environmental Impact

Agriculture contributes to 14% of GHG emissions in India. Kisaan Mitra aims to combat this by:

- **Eliminating Stubble Burning:** Cutting crops closer to the ground to maximize yield and reduce the need for field burning.
- **Air Quality:** Reducing PM2.5 levels, which currently claim over 169,000 children's lives annually in India due to pollution.
- **Soil Health:** Minimizing stubble reduces pest infestations and soil-borne diseases.

## 🤝 Business Models

To ensure accessibility for all income levels, we operate via:

- **B2G (Business to Government):** Collaborating with initiatives like the Krishi Uddhar Scheme.
- **Retail Distribution:** A pan-India network with easy financing options.
- **Leasing Model:** A flexible "Pay-as-you-use" model for the smallest landholders.
